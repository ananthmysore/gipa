A series of talks delivered by Shatavadhani Dr. R. Ganesh on “The Foundations of Indian Culture”.

Ours is a sixty-six year old socio-cultural organization dedicated to the strengthening of cultural roots of the society and promotion of enlightened citizenship. Our Institute was founded in 1945 by the late Padma Bhushan Dr. D. V. Gundappa, doyen of Kannada literature and a savant who strove for over seven decades for the well-being of the society. He was a close collaborator of Sir M. Visvesvaraya, Sri C. Rajagopalachari and other distinguished personages of that generation.
